# Sample Agent Test Questions — Fabric IQ Data Agent

## Overview

This document contains a collection of test questions designed to validate the Fabric Data Agent's capabilities across different scenarios and complexity levels. Questions cover data exploration, business analytics, cross-domain analysis, and supply chain intelligence across the six domain schemas: `customer`, `product`, `sales`, `finance`, `inventory`, and `supplychain`.

Use these questions to validate agent response accuracy before promoting configuration changes to production.

---

## Sample Questions Table

| # | Domain | Question | Notes |
|---|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Customer | Show me the breakdown of customers by customer type (Individual, Business, Government). | Customer type segmentation |
| 2 | Customer | How many customers are associated with each geographic region? | Geographic distribution |
| 3 | Sales | How many line items are typically included in a single order? | Order complexity analysis |
| 4 | Finance | What is the total amount of payments received, broken down by payment method? | Payment receipts summary |
| 5 | Inventory | Which products are currently out of stock or below their reorder point across all warehouses? | Stock alert view |
| 6 | Inventory | What is the total inventory value across all warehouses? | Inventory valuation |
| 7 | Inventory | Show me all open and in-transit purchase orders. When are they expected to arrive and what is each order worth? | Open PO visibility |
| 8 | Inventory | Which warehouse — Main, Backup, or Regional — holds the most available stock? | Warehouse comparison |
| 9 | Supply Chain | Are there any active or monitored supply chain disruptions? What is the severity and estimated impact? | Disruption risk view |
| 10 | Supply Chain | Which products have multiple suppliers available? | Supplier redundancy check |
| 11 | Supply Chain | Which suppliers have the shortest lead times, and how does that compare to our current inventory levels? | Lead time vs stock analysis |
| 12 | Business Analytics | Which product categories generate the most revenue and have the highest profit margins? | Category performance comparison |
| 13 | Business Analytics | Show me the top 5 products by total revenue, including units sold and gross margin. | Top product analysis |
| 14 | Business Analytics | Which products are at risk of running out based on current stock levels and recent sales velocity? | Inventory risk analysis |
